Ferrari 296 GTB (2021): A New Era in Hybrid Performance
Mid-Engine Excellence Meets Cutting-Edge Technology

The Ferrari 296 GTB, introduced in 2021, is a revolutionary mid-engine plug-in hybrid sports car that blends Ferrari's iconic performance with hybrid technology. This model ushers in a new chapter in the brand's storied history, combining thrilling power with eco-conscious innovation.

Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6 + Electric Motor
Total Power: 818 hp (610 kW) combined
Torque: 740 Nm (545 lb-ft) from the V6, with additional torque from the electric motor
Performance: 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) in 2.9 seconds
Top Speed: 330 km/h (205 mph)
Electric Range: Up to 25 km (15.5 miles) on electric power
Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch automatic

Ferrari Daytona SP3 (2022): A Tribute to Racing Heritage
Icona Series Mid-Engine Masterpiece

The Ferrari Daytona SP3, released in 2022, is a limited-production mid-engine sports car that marks the latest entry in Ferrari's prestigious Icona range. This extraordinary vehicle draws inspiration from Ferrari's legendary racing cars of the 1960s, blending retro design with cutting-edge technology.

Engine: 6.5L Naturally Aspirated V12
Horsepower: 829 hp (619 kW) @ 8,500 rpm
Torque: 718 Nm (530 lb-ft) @ 7,250 rpm
Performance: 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) in 2.9 seconds
Top Speed: 340 km/h (211 mph)
Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic


Ferrari Daytona SP3 (2022)






Ferrari Purosangue (2022): The First Four-Door Ferrari
Uniting Luxury, Performance, and Versatility

The Ferrari Purosangue, introduced in 2022, marks a groundbreaking moment in Ferrari's history as the brand's first-ever production four-door vehicle. Combining Ferrari's legendary performance with the practicality of an SUV, the Purosangue sets a new standard for luxury and versatility.

Engine: 6.5L Naturally Aspirated V12
Horsepower: 715 hp (533 kW) @ 7,750 rpm
Torque: 716 Nm (528 lb-ft) @ 6,250 rpm
Performance: 0-100 km/h (0-62 mph) in 3.3 seconds
Top Speed: 310 km/h (193 mph)
Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch automatic


Ferrari Purosangue (2022)
